Meet Me!

I am a creative and result-driven Product Manager with extensive experience in digital products. I have had the privilege of working with Nigeria's top 3 banks building inaugural assets and liability products and thereby contributing to the success of various initiatives, such as Mobile Money (Agent Banking), SME Banking, and Digital Loans across the Retail and SME segments.
Furthermore, I am a co-founder of numerous grant winning Agric-tech startup with the mission of providing portable irrigation systems powered by renewable energy for small-scale farmers in Nigeria.

I strongly believe in the potential of enabling technologies in the SME and Financial industry and so, I am passionate about leveraging technology to drive change, innovation, profitability, and delivering exceptional user satisfaction. In addressing the ever-increasing human problems, I firmly believe that adopting sustainable technologies is the best approach.

Therefore, I am a major proponent of agile principles, with a focus on Scrum, Lean, and Kanban methodologies to drive efficiency, collaboration and innovation in building products, business solutions and research. These approaches are critical for staying ahead in competitive markets and delivering exceptional user satisfaction.

I'm excited to collaborate with you on any discussions related to products, solutions, data, research, and leadership. Whether you need help with ideation, strategy, or problem-solving, feel free to reach out at any time. Let's dive into the world of possibilities and make an impact with technology and sustainable solutions!
